Problem & Solution

The Problem

Reading a large number of emails every day requires significant time and effort.

Our Solution

Tasklytics analyzes emails and sends you a summary along with an Excel file containing the analysis.

Key Benefits

Increased Productivity

Automatically extracts and organizes tasks from emails, saving time and reducing the effort spent manually reviewing messages.

Better Task Management

Clearly identifies pending and completed tasks, responsible persons, and deadlines, helping you stay organized and avoid missed follow-ups.

Improved Accountability & Reporting

Generates structured daily summaries and Excel reports, making it easier to track performance, share updates, and maintain transparency.
